# Checking E-bike Tire Pressure

**System:** Wheel and Tire Assembly  
**Target Pressure:** 3.5 bar (50 PSI)

### ⚠️ SAFETY CRITICAL: READ BEFORE PROCEEDING

| Hazard Symbol | Type | Description |
| :---: | :--- | :--- |
| ![M002](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/70/ISO_7010_M002.svg/100px-ISO_7010_M002.svg.png) | **MANDATORY** | Read the service manual before proceeding. |

* **Visual Inspection:** Examine the tire for visible cuts, bumps, or exposed yellow wear indicators. 
* **Action:** If the tire is damaged, **do not inflate.** Inform your supervisor or contact repair services.

---

### Step-by-Step Instructions

1.  **Remove the valve cap** Turn the cap counter-clockwise and set it aside in a secure location.

2.  **Attach the pump** Connect the air-pump head to the tire valve and lock the lever.

3.  **Adjust the pressure** Observe the pressure gauge:
    * **If pressure is below 3.5 bar:** Move the pump handle up and down until the gauge needle reaches 3.5 bar.
    * **If pressure is above 3.5 bar:** Briefly disconnect the pump or press the bleed valve to release air until the gauge reaches 3.5 bar.
    * **Note:** If the tire does not hold pressure, the tube is damaged. Contact your supervisor.

4.  **Secure the valve** Remove the pump attachment and screw the valve cap back on clockwise until finger-tight.
